About Grand Theater

Our Story, Our Passion, Our Mission

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Grand Theater has been a beacon of culture and entertainment for over 40 years. What started as a small community venue has grown into one of the region's premier performance spaces, hosting thousands of theater-goers annually.

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and entertain our community by presenting world-class performances and fostering a deep appreciation for the theatrical arts. We believe that live theater has the power to transform hearts and minds, and we're committed to making that magic accessible to everyone.

What We Offer

  • 🎭 Professional productions of classic and contemporary plays
  • 🎵 Spectacular musicals and operas
  • 🎪 Comedy nights and variety shows
  • 🩰 Ballet and dance performances
  • 👨‍🎓 Educational programs and workshops
  • 🎟️ Private events and corporate gatherings

Our Venue

Our state-of-the-art theater features:

  • 600+ comfortable seating in the main theater
  • Intimate 150-seat studio theater
  • 100-seat comedy club
  • Professional lighting and sound systems
  • Accessible facilities for all guests
  • On-site café and bar

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of artists, technicians, and support staff work tirelessly to create unforgettable experiences. From our talented actors to our skilled stage crew, every member of the Grand Theater family is passionate about bringing stories to life.

Get Involved

Whether you're a theater enthusiast, aspiring performer, or someone who simply loves the arts, there are many ways to get involved with Grand Theater:

  • Attend performances and become a season subscriber
  • Participate in our workshops and training programs
  • Volunteer as an usher, tech crew member, or helper
  • Become a patron and support our mission
  • Follow us on social media for updates and special offers
